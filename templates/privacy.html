Privacy Policy

To allow university students to login to the website, we require that users sign up using their university email address. When logging in, we collect:

  • Your email
  • Account creation time

We also store your profile settings. All and any post is visible by anyone on the Internet.

We may log requests as you browse the services and store it for 30 days:

  • Your IP address
  • What was requested
  • Was the request a success

The purpose of this data collection is to help us identify any potential abuse.

Cookies

Session cookies ("hithereimacookie") is used to keep track of the user's authentication state. No cookies are used for tracking users.

Third Parties

No information is shared with third parties.

GDPR

You may request to download or delete your data from your profile page.
